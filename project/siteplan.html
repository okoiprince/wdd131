Site Name

B-Cakes

The name "B-Cakes" represents a baking company known for producing delicious, high-quality cakes in Cross River State, Nigeria. It is simple, memorable, and reflects the brand's identity and focus on baked products such as cupcakes, chin-chin and small-chops

Site Purpose

The website will serve as an online platform for B-Cakes to showcase its cake products, provide ordering information, display pricing, and allow customers to easily contact the business. It will also highlight special offers and custom cake services.

Scenarios

  • What types of cakes does B-Cakes offer for birthdays and weddings?
  • How can I place an order or contact B-Cakes for a custom cake?

Color Schema

  • Pink (#ff6f91): Used for headings, buttons, and highlights.
  • Cream (#fff5e1): Used for background to create a soft, warm feel.

Typography

  • Roboto: Used as primary font for all text.
  • Sans-serif: Used as alternative font for all text.
