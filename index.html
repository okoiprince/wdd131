Cross River, Nigeria

Cross River State is located in southeastern Nigeria and is known for its rich cultural heritage diverse ethnic groups, and abundant natural attractions. It is home to Calabar, a historic city famous for tourism, festivals and hospitality. The state is also noted for its rainforests, wildlife reserves, and role as one of Nigeria’s leading tourism destinations.
